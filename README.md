# 📊 Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard developed using Google Sheets. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing sales, profit, quantity sold, product categories, sub-categories, and payment modes. Through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking, the dashboard helps identify trends, evaluate business performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.
- Evaluate customer payment preferences.
- Monitor order volume and quantity sold.
- Generate meaningful business insights through data visualization.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains transaction-level sales data with the following fields:

- Order ID
- Amount
- Profit
- Quantity
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Payment Mode

**Dataset Source:** Kaggle

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Orders

---

## Dashboard Features

### 📌 Sales by Category
A pie chart displaying the contribution of each product category to total sales.

### 📌 Top Sub-Categories
A bar chart highlighting the best-performing sub-categories based on sales amount.

### 📌 Profit by Category
A column chart comparing profitability across product categories.

### 📌 Payment Mode Analysis
A donut chart illustrating the distribution of sales across different payment methods.
